All About Us

We are a family-owned and operated ice cream shop located in Montfort, Wisconsin. For almost 20 years, we've been serving our community delicious scoops of Chocolate Shoppe Ice Cream - a local favorite from Madison, Wisconsin that's been churning out premium ice cream for over 50 years.

Our ice cream selection includes classic flavors like vanilla and chocolate, as well as more adventurous options like Zanzibar Chocolate, Fat Elvis, and Superman. And when the colder months roll around, we bring in seasonal flavors like Pumpkin, Eggnog, and Caramel Apple Pie. We also offer gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegan flavors.




More Than Just Sundaes

At Grab A Cone, our story goes beyond ice cream. While we're proud to be known for our delicious ice creams, our commitment to satisfying our community extends further. We offer a diverse range of treats, including teas, coffee, smoothie bowls, homemade banana bread, cookies, and a selection of local goods. If you're in the mood for something more substantial, check out our menu of sandwiches and burgers, made with fresh, local bread. Additionally, we offer a weekly rotating homemade soup and weekly meal specials, ensuring there's always something new to try!
